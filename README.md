# 🐾 Petty: Your One-Stop Pet Food Ecommerce Platform

Welcome to **Petty**! 🎉 This project, created as part of the **1st Semester Mini Project** at **CMRIT Bangalore**, is an ecommerce platform dedicated to pet food for a variety of pets such as dogs, cats, fish, hamsters, rabbits, and ducks. With user-friendly features like filters, multi-image selection, and seamless "Buy Now" and "Add to Cart" options, Petty aims to make shopping for your furry, feathered, or finned friends a breeze! 🐶🐱🐟🐰🦆

## 🚀 Features

1. **Pet Food Categories**: Explore food options for a wide range of pets: 
   - 🐶 Dogs  
   - 🐱 Cats  
   - 🐟 Fish  
   - 🐹 Hamsters  
   - 🐰 Rabbits  
   - 🦆 Ducks  

2. **Filter Options**: Easily filter products to find the perfect fit for your pet.

3. **Multi-Image Selection**: View products from multiple angles for better decision-making.

4. **Buy Now & Add to Cart**: Seamless shopping experience with direct purchase and cart options.
